Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve all major cities in Cyprus including Larnaca, Nicosia, Limassol, and Paphos. Additional travel fees may apply for locations outside city centers.

What's included in a standard cleaning?

Our standard cleaning includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming/mopping floors, cleaning bathrooms and kitchen, emptying trash, and making beds. We can customize based on your specific needs.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, our team comes fully equipped with professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment. If you prefer us to use specific products you provide, just let us know in advance.

How do you determine pricing?

Prices are based on the size of your space, type of service requested, and frequency of cleaning. We offer discounts for recurring bookings and student customers.

Are your cleaners insured?

Yes, all our cleaners are fully insured and bonded. Your property is protected in the rare case of any damage occurring during cleaning.

How do I pay for services?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is due after service completion unless you've arranged a different payment schedule.
